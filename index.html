Who We Are

Allflo Technlogies Inc. Vancouver BC ship repair company was founded in 1999 with vast experience in ship repair, new construction, conversions of ships and offshore industries. It is a 24/7 mobile operation: Dockside harbour, and voyage repairs at any location in canada. Our teams do the on voyage repair work. We undertake with our experience and expertise all structural, mechanical, and electrical repairs of vessels. We provide accountability, reliability, and excellent service to our clients in the best possible manner. Allflo Technologies Inc. has developed a reputation for a quality and prompt response for all kinds of services provided to our valued customers.

Vessel steel repairs, fabrication, pipework, main and auxiliary engine overhaul, turbocharger overhaul, crane and cargo gear repairs, winch repairs, electrical motor repairs, mechanical and hydraulic repairs, and we specialize in boiler repairs. We are a complete machine shop and also specialize in internal anti-corrosive coatings for pipes and tanks. Hydraulic repair and re-manufacturing of pumps, motors, valves and winches, electronic and hydraulic control systems related to all deck and engine room machinery. We undertake all bow thruster overhauling and repair work.
